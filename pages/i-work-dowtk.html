Day Out With The Kids is a leading platform for finding family adventure, whatever the weather, wherever you are in the country. With a strong base of 17million users, Halo were appointed to re-imagine the brand, shaping it's proposition , developing the digital experience to ignite growth and expand market share in the UK.

User-centric platform

With a focus on mobile-up, Halo developed an intuitive search and discover approach that seamlessly connects families to exciting days out and adventures of every shape and size, prioritising location and relevance. Clear sign-posting navigation and advanced filtering return timely and personalised results.

We also developed a detailed - and ambitious - content strategy to help grow a loyal community of influencers and contributors, creating greater value for every user engagement.
