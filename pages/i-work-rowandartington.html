A new perspective for wealth management

A new perspective for wealth management

Client:
Rowan Dartington

Work:
Brand Narrative / Strategy / Campaign / Digital

Rowan Dartington are a progressive wealth management business & leading brand in the St. James’s Place portfolio. With an international footprint, Rowan Dartington are highly regarded as one of the most advanced & instinctive players in a famously competitive industry.

We developed the brand positioning strategy & narrative platform with a detailed architecture and strategic global plan for B2B & B2C communications. This became the foundation for an extensive visual rebrand, including identity, complete art direction & graphic language alongside messaging hierarchy & tone-of-voice.

See the world again

Our art direction focuses on a simple premise based in the research and analytical ability of Rowan Dartington.
